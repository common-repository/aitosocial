== Installation ==

Installing AItoSocial is straightforward and can be completed in a few steps:

1. Download the plugin from wordpress.org, unpack it, and upload the <strong>[aitosocial]</strong> folder to your <strong>wp-content/plugins/</strong> directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Navigate to <strong>Dashboard > AItoSocial > Settings</strong> to customize and manage your social media content.

Enjoy the simplicity and efficiency of AItoSocial!
